Why us.

History

Amalfi Tiles, previously known as Cass Tiles, has been servicing the Eastern Suburbs selling tiles and tiling supplies for over 50 years. Amalfi Tiles was then created in 2011 offering the building and design industry a range of tiles, stone and bathroom accessories from around the globe. Being a family owned business guarantees you a service at Amalfi like no other.

Quality

Every product that enters our store is vigorously tested for quality and durability. We have access to an excellent range of tap ware, tiles, vanities and more. There is no limit to what we can design, we often work closely with clients to assist with choosing the right finishes for their space. We can design and install your kitchen and joinery and deliver time and time again with the latest designer bathroom products.

Expertise

Our trade centre is the heart of our business and provides tilers and builders with adhesive, grouts, self-levelling, waterproofing, grate and tile education. We offer regular trade nights for our tilers and builders connecting the latest tiling solutions with the products we sell.
